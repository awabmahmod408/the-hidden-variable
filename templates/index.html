Silhouette is computed on non-noise points (sampled to 1500 max) using haversine distance. Higher is better; range [-1, 1].

Forecast new cluster locations

Fits a weighted Gaussian KDE over existing DBSCAN cluster centroids (weights = log(cluster size)), evaluates density on an expanded bounding box, and selects peaks that sit at least min_dist metres away from any existing cluster. Non-max suppression enforces spacing between picks. Results are drawn on the Discovery Map as purple ★ markers (new layer).
